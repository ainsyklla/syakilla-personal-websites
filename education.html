'*•.¸♡ SK BERTAM ♡¸.•*'

My educational journey at SK Bertam spanned from Year 1 to Year 6. During my time at the school, I had the opportunity to expand my social network and interact with individuals from diverse age groups. In Year 5, I undertook the UPKK (Ujian Penilaian Kelas Kafa) examination, and subsequently, in Year 6, I sat for the UPSR, which is considered the most significant primary school examination. This chapter of my education is replete with cherished memories, particularly my attainment of a co-curricular award and my participation in a state-level co-curricular camp alongside three peers. Furthermore, I actively engaged in various sports activities at both school and zone levels.

'*•.¸♡ SMK TENGKU BENDAHARA ♡¸.•*'

Throughout my five-year tenure in secondary school, I actively engaged in a wide array of school activities and events, significantly contributing to my personal development and leadership acumen. Commencing as a hostel committee member in Form 1, I progressively shouldered greater responsibilities, including supporting teachers during school events and participating in community initiatives such as the dengue prevention program. In my final year, Form 5, I had the honor of being appointed as a school prefect and assistant hostel prefect. Notably, my Form 5 year was concurrent with the unprecedented challenges posed by the COVID-19 pandemic, presenting unique obstacles and valuable insights. Furthermore, during my secondary school tenure, I confronted two pivotal examinations: the PT3 in Form 3 and the SPM in Form 5. Reflecting on my secondary school experiences, I hold the memories and lessons dear, as they have profoundly influenced my character.
